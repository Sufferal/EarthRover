Overview

Tomato is a popular fruit/vegetable (botanically it's a fruit, but often considered a vegetable in culinary contexts) known for its versatility and nutritional value.

Diseases

Tomatoes are rich in vitamins, particularly vitamin C and vitamin K. They also contain lycopene, an antioxidant that may help reduce the risk of certain cancers.

Details

Here are some delicious tomato recipes:

  • Caprese Salad
  • Tomato Basil Pasta
  • Tomato Soup
  • Bruschetta

Varieties

There are numerous varieties of tomatoes, including Beefsteak, Roma, Cherry, and Heirloom, each with its own unique flavor and texture.
